\# Project Constitution



Purpose

\- Provide a small, maintainable service that aggregates weather, news and social links for learning/demo purposes.



Scope

\- In-scope: API endpoints, integration tests, documentation, CI.

\- Out-of-scope: long-term data retention, heavy production telemetry.



Roles \& Responsibilities

\- Maintainer(s): review and merge changes, release tags.

\- Contributors: create issues, open PRs, follow contribution rules.



Decision Making

\- Maintainters have final say for architectural and breaking changes.

\- Non-breaking changes: merged after 1 approving review and green CI.

\- Breaking changes: require 2 maintainers' approval.



Contribution Process

\- Use feature branches named feature/<short-desc> or fix/<short-desc>.

\- Open PRs targeting main; include description and tests.

\- Run/ensure CI passes and address review comments.



Roadmap

\- Short-term: stabilize endpoints, add integration tests, add Spekit content.

\- Long-term: authentication, rate limiting, production hardening.



Review Cycle

\- Review this constitution every 6 months or on major project milestone.



Contact

\- Open issues in this repo or tag maintainers in PRs.
